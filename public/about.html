About Us

Our Mission

At AutoSend Pro, we believe in the power of direct and effective communication. Our mission is to empower small businesses and entrepreneurs to reach their customers with ease and efficiency by providing a WhatsApp marketing tool that combines simplicity with power.

What is AutoSend Pro?

AutoSend Pro is a carefully designed desktop application that helps you automate your WhatsApp marketing campaigns. Instead of wasting hours sending messages manually, you can now set up your campaign in minutes, import contacts from an Excel file, and send personalized messages and images to thousands of customers with a single click.

Why Choose Us?

We focus on delivering a practical and straightforward solution that solves a real problem. Our software does not require complex technical expertise; it is designed to be user-friendly for everyone while maintaining the high performance and reliability you need to grow your business.
